About Me

Hello! My name is Benn Gallagher. I am a 25 year old web developer in training, currently studying at the George Washington University. This page is where you can find all my info about who I am, what I’m doing, and where to get a hold of me.


I attended George Mason University from 2014-2018 where I earned my undergrad in government and international politics. During my time at George Mason I pursued my interests in areas of study ranging from computer science, to international relations. As part of my International relations requirements I, along with my class, attended a session of the United Nations in New York City. There we met our associate professor Ahmad Kamal, who served as the United Nation’s representative from Pakistan in the mid to late 1990’s.


Sports have been a huge part of my life since I was a little kid. I played travel basketball and soccer my entire childhood, but by the time I reached high school I had shifted one hundred percent of my focus to swimming. From ages 5-18 I swam competitively for a bunch of different swim clubs; and many of the later years I swam year round. I swam for my high school’s varsity team all four years and was a coach for my summer team. Since my swimming days I have pursued a number of new sports including rock climbing and boxing, as well as returning to my true love, basketball.
